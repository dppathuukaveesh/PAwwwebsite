About Our Planet

Position in Solar System

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. This is enabled by Earth being a water world.

Atmosphere & Life

The atmosphere of Earth is composed of nitrogen and oxygen. It protects us from meteoroids and harmful solar radiation.

Diverse Ecosystems

From the depths of the oceans to the highest mountains, Earth supports a vast variety of ecosystems and living organisms.

Planetary Structure

The Layers

  • Crust The outermost solid shell of Earth, ranging from 5-70 km in depth.
  • Mantle The widest section of Earth, composed of silicate rocks rich in magnesium and iron.
  • Outer Core Liquid layer composed of iron and nickel.
  • Inner Core Solid center composed primarily of iron.
